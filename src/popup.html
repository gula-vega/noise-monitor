🔊 QuietCue

Monitors your microphone on Google Meet and Microsoft Teams, and warns you if you speak too loudly.

How to Use

  • Open a Google Meet or Microsoft Teams call.
  • Allow microphone access when prompted.
  • Adjust the Volume Threshold slider in the top-left corner of the call tab.
  • A red overlay appears when your voice exceeds the threshold.
  • Switch tabs freely — a red ! badge and tab title warning keep you informed.

Tips

  • Default threshold is −23 dB.
  • Move the slider left (more negative) to be less sensitive.
  • Move the slider right (closer to 0) to be more sensitive.
  • Click ON/OFF to pause monitoring without uninstalling.
  • The overlay does not block clicks — you can still use your call normally.

This extension processes audio locally only. No audio is recorded or transmitted. Your settings are synced via Chrome storage.
